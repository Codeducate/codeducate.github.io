Purpose

Codeducate was founded by a team of students from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology determined to foster computer science among middle school students.

How do we plan to do this?

  • Educate students in various aspects of computer science, including web development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) and algorithmic programming (Python and Java).
  • Inform students about the benefits of computer science, including its importance in the real world and its applications.
  • Guest speakers: we'll have 2 to 3 guest speakers throughout the year from local startups and tech companies. They'll talk about the cool things they've done!
  • And finally... work on a year-long project! The students will work on a project using the skills they've learned and present it at the end of the year.
