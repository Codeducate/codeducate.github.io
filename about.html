Purpose

Codeducate was founded by a team of students from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology determined to foster computer science among middle school students.

How do we plan to do this?

  • Help the students build their very own website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This will be our main focus. We'll work on this throughout the year!
  • Inform students about the benefits of computer science. This includes its importance in the real world (the problems it has solved, etc.) and how we, as students, use it at jobs (internships), in class, and at home.
  • Guest speakers - we'll have 3 to 5 guest speakers throughout the year from local startups and tech companies. They'll talk about the cool things they've done!
  • And finally... work on a year-long project! The students will work on a project using the skills they've learned and present it at the end of the year. We'll have cool prizes for everyone!
