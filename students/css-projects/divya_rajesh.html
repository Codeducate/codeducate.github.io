Fun Facts about Cheetahs

By:Divya Rajesh:)

Cheetahs are the fastest mammals in the world!

Cheetahs are the only big cats that can turn in mid-air while sprinting!

They make distinct facial expressions to signal their mood!

The cheetahs originated over 4 million years ago, thats longer than any of the other big cats today! While running a cheetah uses its tail to steer; like a rudder for a boat! The cheetah is hard to see because its spotted coat blends with the tall, dry grass of the plains! After a chase a cheetah needs half an hour to rest before it eats!

They can only purr not roar like lions! They weigh 45-60 kilograms!

Its body temperature can reach up to 105 degrees Fahrenheit! A cheetah's respitory rate climbs from 60 to 150 breaths per minute during a high speed chase!

Cheetahs only need to drink once every three to four days
